The ceremony and reception are in Gaithersburg, Maryland. Without traffic, it is less than an hour from Washington, DC. We would recommend Dulles or National airport. Dulles is a bit closer. Gaithersburg has a metro stop (about 4 miles from the hotel) but you will need a car to get around Gaithersburg.

The hotel is the Gaithersburg Marriott Washingtonian Center. There is a group rate for Saturday and Sunday night. The hotel is about 5 mile from Smokey Glen Farm. We are not planning on a shuttle between the hotel and the wedding.
